Research

I work on robotics methods that are reliable under distribution shift and real-world uncertainty. My current interests include uncertainty-aware policies for contact-rich manipulation, sim-to-real adaptation, and robust navigation under partial observability.

Robot learning for manipulation

Learning policies for contact-rich tasks using RL/IL, with structured priors and uncertainty estimation.

  • Grasping, insertion, tool use
  • Offline RL, imitation learning
  • Safety constraints & failure recovery

Sim-to-real transfer

Improving generalization from simulation by aligning representations and adapting dynamics online.

  • Domain randomization & adaptation
  • Self-supervised perception
  • Benchmarking & evaluation

Autonomy & navigation

Robust navigation with learned perception and planning, including risk-aware decision-making.

  • Semantic mapping
  • Trajectory optimization
  • Risk-aware control
